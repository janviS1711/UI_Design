paragraph is a distinct section of writing, typically consisting of several sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It is organized to present a unified thought, often starting with a topic sentence, followed by supporting details, and ending with a concluding sentence that summarizes or transitions to the next paragraph. Paragraphs are essential for structuring written work, making it easier for the reader to follow and understand
